Sustainability

We are a business that operates
responsibly and sustainably

Sustainability is important to us and to all of our stakeholders. We serve, supply and
protect those who serve and protect us, in a corporate culture that is performance driven
and values led. Our sustainability agenda supports this purpose

Our approach to
sustainability

What does sustainability and ESG mean to us?

Environment

The risk that climate change will have a significant and lasting impact on global economic growth and prosperity is a factor that we recognise and
are responding to. Our target is to achieve net zero greenhouse gas emissions across our operations by 2030
